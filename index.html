How an engagement runs

Four phases. No surprises in month six.

Phase 1

Discovery

Current-state assessment, stakeholder interviews, and an honest read on where the program actually stands.

Phase 2

Mobilize

Scope, roadmap, dependency map, and a RACI that people actually follow. Risks named out loud, early.

Phase 3

Deliver

Execution with weekly cadence, vendor coordination, and reporting your leadership can read in five minutes.

Phase 4

Stabilize

Cutover support, knowledge transfer, and a clean handoff — not a disappearance the day the contract ends.

Why Cybaxis

Most firms sell you a deck. We sell you delivery.

Cybaxis was founded in 2021 on a simple observation: technology programs rarely fail because of the technology. They fail because of unclear ownership, invisible dependencies, and plans nobody updates after week two.

Our background spans airline operations, insurance platforms, and government modernization — environments where downtime costs real money and "we'll fix it next sprint" isn't an answer.
