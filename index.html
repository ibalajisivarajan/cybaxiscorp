How a search runs

Four steps. Shortlist in days, not weeks.

Step 1

Intake

A quick call to understand the role, tech stack, team, and start-date pressure.

Step 2

Shortlist (48–72 hrs)

Pre-vetted candidates matched to your stack and delivered fast.

Step 3

Interview

Step 4

Place & Support

Offer support, onboarding help, and check-ins after the start date.

Why Cybaxis

Most firms flood your inbox with resumes. We send a shortlist worth reading.

Cybaxis was founded in 2021 to fix a specific problem in IT staffing: too much volume, not enough vetting. We built a leaner process — direct sourcing, real technical screening, and shortlists you can act on in days, not weeks.

Our placements span aviation, insurance, fintech, and professional services — industries where a bad hire, or an empty seat, costs real money.
