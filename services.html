Services

Four ways we place IT talent.

Some clients need a contractor for three months. Others need a permanent hire they won't have to replace. Either way, we start the same: understand the role, source with intent, and vet before we send anyone your way.

01 — Contract Staffing

Contract Staffing

For short-to-medium term IT needs — a project ramp-up, a leave coverage, a deadline you can't push. Talent ready to start fast.

01

Rapid sourcing

Pre-vetted contractors matched to your stack and your start date.

02

Fast onboarding

Ramped and productive within days, not weeks.

03

Flexible terms

Scale contract length up or down as the need shifts.

04

Ongoing support

We stay involved for the life of the contract, not just the placement.

02 — Contract-to-Hire

Contract-to-Hire

Bring someone on as a contractor, evaluate real on-the-job fit, and convert to permanent when it works for both sides.

01

Trial period

Evaluate real performance before committing long-term.

02

Clear conversion terms

Transparent timeline and terms agreed up front.

03

Continuity

The same person who joined the team is the one who stays.

04

No re-search cost

Skip a second search and onboarding cycle at conversion.

03 — Direct Placement

Direct Placement

Permanent hire search for long-term builds — when you know the role is here to stay and you want it filled right the first time.

01

Role scoping

We get specific on the role before we source, so shortlists stay tight.

02

Deep candidate vetting

Technical screening plus a real read on team fit.

03

Shortlist in days

A short, qualified list, not a stack of unscreened resumes.

04

Offer support

Help closing the offer, not just presenting the candidate.

04 — Executive & Niche Search

Executive & Niche Search

Specialized and leadership IT roles, where the candidate pool is small and the search has to be precise.

01

Targeted search

Direct outreach to strong candidates who aren't actively job-hunting.

02

Leadership vetting

Assessed for scope, judgment, and org fit, not just technical skills.

03

Confidential process

Discreet search for sensitive or replacement hires.

04

Niche technical roles

Hard-to-fill specialist roles, sourced from a senior bench.
