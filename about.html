About Cybaxis

Most staffing firms send resumes. We send people who are ready to start Monday.

Cybaxis Corp is an IT staffing firm serving hiring companies across the USA, Canada, and India since 2021.

Our story

Why Cybaxis exists

Cybaxis Corp was founded in 2021 to fix a specific problem in IT staffing: too many firms send volume, not vetted talent. We built a leaner process — direct sourcing, real technical screening, and shortlists hiring managers can act on in days, not weeks.

We staff contract, contract-to-hire, and permanent IT roles for enterprise teams across industries where an empty seat, or the wrong hire, is expensive.

What we believe

Three principles that shape every search.

Vetted, not just available

A candidate on the market isn't the same as a candidate who's right for your role. We screen for both.

Senior bench, not a resume pile

We maintain relationships with strong candidates before you need them, not a database we search cold.

Fast doesn't mean careless

A 48–72 hour shortlist is still a fully vetted one. Speed is a process, not a shortcut.
