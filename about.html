About Cybaxis

Built by someone who's sat on your side of the table.

Cybaxis Corp is an IT consulting firm based in British Columbia, Canada, serving clients across Canada and the United States since 2021.

Our story

Why Cybaxis exists

The firm was built on 15+ years of hands-on delivery experience inside large enterprises: airline operations and cloud modernization at a major US carrier, platform programs in insurance and financial services, and modernization work in the public sector.

That background shapes how we consult. We've sat on the client side of the table, inherited failing vendor engagements, and cleaned up after consultants who disappeared once the slides were done. We built Cybaxis to be the firm we wished we'd hired.

What we believe

Three principles that shape every engagement.

Honest plans, early

We'd rather tell you the uncomfortable truth in week one than in month six. A plan is only useful if it's honest.

Senior over scale

Small senior teams beat large junior ones, every time. The person you meet in the sales call does the work.

Tools serve process

Tools don't fix broken processes. But the right tools, adopted well, make good processes measurably faster.
